START PLANNING YOUR M.I.C.E TRIPS ON INDIA'S MOST COMPREHENSIVE MARKETPLACE

Effortless, budget-friendly and limitless choices — just one click away!

MICEkart.com is the first and only comprehensive online marketplace solution for Corporate Travel and Events, specifically Meetings, Incentives, Conferences and Exhibitions (M.I.C.E.) products & services. It was developed by Gavin DCosta and Abhishek Gupta with the aim of creating value for buyers and suppliers of M.I.C.E solutions and simplifying the way the industry conducts M.I.C.E. business.

Our Groundbreaking technological Innovations
Transforming the M.I.C.E Industry

MICEkart.com bridges the gap between M.I.C.E. solution providers & customers across the globe by offering them a unique, easy-to-use single window platform where they can transact with each other directly. It provides sellers with enhanced global reach and an additional, effective sales channel, while allowing buyers to plan, purchase, and execute their events effortlessly.

MICEkart Event App – A Smart Mobile Solution for Corporate Travel and Event Management The MICEkart Event App is designed to streamline corporate travel and event execution, offering a seamless experience for both organizers and delegates. Key features include:

Paperless Document Management

Effortlessly manage tickets, itineraries, destination details, hotel information, and more—all in one place.

QR Code Scanning

Simplify delegate tagging and attendance management with quick response (QR) code scanning.

Express Check-in

Enjoy hassle-free check-ins at event venues and hotels for a smooth start to your experience.

Event Alerts & Reminders

Stay on track with real-time alerts, reminders, and schedulers to ensure a seamless event flow.

SOS Feature

Prioritize delegate safety with instant access to emergency assistance while traveling.

Client Profiling

Deliver personalized services tailored to each delegate’s preferences and needs.

In-app Chat

Foster connectivity among delegates with an integrated chat feature, keeping them in touch while on the go.

Photo - Video Gallery

Capture and share event moments through the in-app photo and video gallery for a lasting memory.
